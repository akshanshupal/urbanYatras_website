Cancellation & Refund Policy

At Urban Yatras, we strive to provide a smooth and enjoyable travel experience for our customers. Please read our cancellation and refund policy carefully before confirming your booking.

1. Cancellation by Customer
Cancellation Requests

Customers may request cancellation of their booking by contacting our customer support team or by emailing us at support@urbanyatras.com.

Cancellation Charges

The following cancellation charges will apply based on the number of days remaining before the trip departure date:

Token Amount:
The token amount paid at the time of booking is non-refundable. However, it may be adjusted against a future booking within 365 days from the original booking date.
45 Days or More Before Departure:
70% of the total package amount will be refunded.
30% of the package cost will be charged as cancellation fees.
30 Days Before Departure:
50% of the total package amount will be refunded.
50% cancellation charges will apply.
15 Days Before Departure:
30% of the total package amount will be refunded.
70% cancellation charges will apply.
Within 7 Days of Departure:
No refund will be applicable.
Post Booking Confirmation:
Certain bookings may become non-refundable immediately after confirmation due to supplier or operational commitments.
Festive & Peak Season Bookings:
No refunds will be applicable during festive seasons, long weekends, peak travel periods, or special event dates.
2. Cancellation by Urban Yatras
Land Packages

In rare situations where Urban Yatras is unable to operate a confirmed trip due to unavoidable circumstances, operational issues, or supplier unavailability, customers will be informed promptly. In such cases, we will offer:

a suitable alternative arrangement, or
a full refund for the affected services.
Transportation Bookings (Flight / Train / Bus)
For Tatkal train bookings, if the ticket remains unconfirmed, the applicable train booking amount will be refunded.
For flight or bus booking disruptions, we will make reasonable efforts to provide alternative travel options of similar value and availability.
3. Refund Policy
Refund Processing Timeline

All eligible refunds will be processed after verification and approval of the applicable cancellation conditions. Refunds are generally processed within 15 to 45 working days from the date of approval.

Please note that the time taken for the refunded amount to reflect in your account may vary depending on your bank, payment gateway, or payment method.

4. Important Notes
Refunds, if any, will be processed through the original mode of payment.
Convenience fees, payment gateway charges, and taxes (if applicable) may be non-refundable.
Urban Yatras reserves the right to amend this policy at any time without prior notice.
